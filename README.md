# VelocityPulse - Marketing & Planning

> Your network's heartbeat, at a glance

Marketing and product planning for VelocityPulse - a SaaS network monitoring
product by Velocity EU. Transform IT Dashboard into a commercial multi-tenant
platform.

## Product Vision

**VelocityPulse** is infrastructure monitoring for everyone - not just enterprise.
Auto-discovery, real-time dashboards, zero complexity. The anti-Nagios.

## Key Decisions

| Decision | Choice |
|----------|--------|
| Product Name | VelocityPulse |
| Tagline | "Your network's heartbeat, at a glance" |
| Launch | Global from day one |
| Free Tier | 50 devices forever |
| Billing | Monthly rate, billed annually |
| Payment | Stripe + Apple Pay |

## Pricing (Proposed)

| Tier | Price | Devices | Agents |
|------|-------|---------|--------|
| Free Forever | £0 | 50 | 1 |
| Pro | £29/mo | 200 | 5 |
| Business | £79/mo | 500 | 10 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | Unlimited |

*All paid tiers billed annually. 30-day free Pro trial available.*

## Target Markets

- SMB IT Teams (1-5 staff)
- UK Schools & Multi-Academy Trusts
- Managed Service Providers (MSPs)
- Small businesses without IT departments

## Positioning

**"Enterprise monitoring at startup prices"**

- 10-minute onboarding (vs days for Nagios/Zabbix)
- Auto-discovery (no manual configuration)
- Real-time by default (not polling)
- Built for non-experts

## The Anti-Nagios Manifesto

We believe:
- Monitoring shouldn't require a PhD
- Setup should take minutes, not days
- Your dashboard should explain itself
- Pricing should be a no-brainer, not a negotiation

**Our promise:**
> "From signup to monitoring in under 10 minutes. No consultants required."

## Go-to-Market Timeline

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-2)
- Finalize multi-tenant architecture
- Implement OAuth (Microsoft, Google, Apple)
- Set up Stripe billing
- Create marketing website

### Phase 2: Soft Launch (Months 3-4)
- Beta program with 10-20 customers
- Collect testimonials and case studies
- Create demo videos and documentation

### Phase 3: Public Launch (Months 5-6)
- ProductHunt launch
- UK education sector outreach
- MSP partner program
- Content marketing
